Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is this game?

Answer: This is an arcade style math game.

Question 2: How do you score points?

Answer: You score points by solving mathematical expressions. You have three choices, and must select the correct one

Question 3: Do you have to create an account?

Answer: No. Best of all, this game can be played without signing up.

Question 4: Do you have to pay to play this game?

Answer: Unlike most games, this one is completely free.
