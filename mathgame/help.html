How To Play

In this math game, you are given two expressions. Your objective is to compare the two expressions. Determine if the first expression is larger, equal to or less than the second expression.

4 + 5
1 + 3

In this case, the first expression is greater. Click the greater than button.

2 + 4
5 + 1

Here, both expressions equal 6. You click on the equals button

2 + 1
3 + 7

The left hand expression is lower than the right hand expression. Click less than
